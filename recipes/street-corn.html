Mexican Street Corn

Credit: Soup Loving Nicole at Allrecipes

Description

Mexican street corn is a quick, easy dish that hits the mark for both sweet and savory. Mayonnaise adds a creamy body, while lime juice and cilantro give it a little zing. Chili powder rounds out the dish by adding a touch of heat without overwhelming the other flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1½ teaspoons chili powder
  • 4 ears sweet corn, shucked and kernels cut off
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro
  • 3 tablespoons cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, lime juice, and chili powder together in a bowl until evenly combined. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add corn and cook for 5 minutes without stirring. Toss around with a spatula and cook until corn has started to brown, about 3 minutes more. Remove from heat.
  3. Add reserved mayonnaise mixture and stir until evenly combined. Add cilantro and stir again. Transfer to serving plates and sprinkle cotija cheese over the top.
